  • antispam

    Manages antispam settings.

    Commands: antispamignore, antispam.

    Usage: !antispamignore {channel_id}, !antispam {threshold}.

  • autorole

    Assigns roles automatically when a user joins.

    Commands: autorole.

    Usage: !autorole {rolename}.

  • ban

    Bans a user from the server.

    Commands: ban, unban, banlist.

    Usage: !ban {user}, !unban {userid}, !banlist.

  • blacklist

    Adds users or commands to the blacklist.

    Commands: blacklist.

    Usage: !blacklist {user}.

    Commands: clear.

    Usage: !clear {number}.
